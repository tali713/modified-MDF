General Info

Masterwork DF is a huge mod-pack that combines several major mods (playable races), minor mods (items, creatures, workshops), tilesets, utilities and dfhack scripts to one pack. Everything is pre-installed and it comes with its own launcher: The MasterworkDF GUI. It allows you to install or deinstall any part of the mod with a simple button click. In short: This is the StarterPack on crack!

The entire vanilla DF content is still there and unchanged, which makes the mod easy to get into.

Currently you can select one of five races to play: Dwarves, Kobolds, Orcs, Succubi and the Hermit. First one are the metalworkers and smiths you know and love; Kobolds are more tribal in nature, stay on the surface and rely on leather and wood as build materials; Orcs are both war-mongering steppe warriors as well as proud craftsmen and artisans; Succubi are evil demons that convert prisoners to their cause and summon monsters; while the Hermit mode allows you to play a single unit in fortress mode out of 200 intelligent races, be it Ogre, Bat Man, Goblin or Grimeling.

Please skim through the rest of the manual to get an idea of the mod content. :)

WORKSHOPS

Gemcutter's Workshop -

Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Architect & Gemcutter Gem Cutting 2 Blocks 5x3 Mason or Carpenter

You can make furniture from gems here. Just treat this as a mason that uses gems instead of boulders. All reactions need one gem, except the gem anvil, which needs three.

Pottery -

Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Architect & Mason Stonecrafting & Pottery 2 Blocks 5x3 Mason or Carpenter

The Pottery shapes and dries clay items. You can make clay, fire clay and kaolinite items from the collected raw materials, resulting in greenware items. Greenware is a special name for wet clay. The workshop will automatically queue two drying reactions, from greenware to leather-hard, from leather-hard to bone-dry. The items can not be used until they are fired in the Clay Oven.

To make it simpler: Shape Item => Dry => Dry => Fire Item.

The material progression is: Clay => Greenware => Leather-Hard => Bone-Dry => Ceramic

Each reaction has a 5% chance to fail, simulating impurities in the clay.

Sawmill -

Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Architect & Lumberjack Woodcutting 2 Blocks, 2 Mechanisms, 1 Saw Blade 5x3 Mason & Mechanic & Forge

 Each kind of tree in the mod can be planted in farm plots, if you are in the correct biome and have the acorns or spores. Trees, Ironbark, Steeloak, Fungiwood, Sporetrees, Crystal Wood, Glassthorns, Nethercaps, Shadethorn, Feather and Glumprong can be farmed. Seeds must be brought at embark, bought from caravans, or discovered from cutting collected tree saplings.

All trees grow all year and need at least one year to mature. The tree saplings are treated as plants and stored as such. Only the Sawmill can cut them into wood logs.

Soul forge -


Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Weaponsmith Weaponsmith 2 Blocks, 2 Mechanisms, 1 Saw Blade 4x3 Well of souls & Forge

Soul forges allow you to turn the soul of the dead into powerful weapons. 

You can turn a pitchfork into a stygian pitchfork. Those upgraded weapons hit with a lot more weight and double as a long range weapon, firing spell shards.You can also make special shards that cause pain, explosion or necrosis on impact.

Some equipments taken from invaders can be defiled here. Clean, non decorated breastplates, greaves or shields can be improved at the cost of one soul. It will also be resized for your race's needs. The quality increase is linked to the worker's skill, a legendary +15 provides a garanteed masterwork.

STUDIES

Archeologist's Study -

Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Architect & Archeologist Archeology 5 Blocks,1 Journal, 1 Abacus, 1 Hourglass 5x3 Mason or Carpenter, Craftsdwarf, Miners to find fossils

The Archeologist uses found fossils, relics and treasures to create random rewards. Fossils are single tiles in rock. Ruined brick veins have a higher chance of containing any type of relics and treasure.

Fossils actually count as gems, but please do not cut them in the Jeweller, it makes them unusable to the Archeologist. All reactions are run automatically, your Archeologist will pick up the fossil and choose the right reaction for you.

Attorney's stock -


Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Architect Negotiation 4 Blocks 5x5 None

The attorney's stock opens a link to the warlocks and allow to trade with them using souls as a currency. In this building, you can buy research breakthroughs and other items required by other workshops such as the gemforge and the glassforge.

You can also conjure slade anvils and boulders from copper bars to answer tricky situations involving nobles and hasty embarks.

RITUALS

Temples provides many upgrades and rituals for your succubi, providing upgrades or affecting the site as a whole. Many of those jobs requires souls that are harvested from dead creatures (see the well of souls below).

Rituals sharing a similar goals are attached to a theme:
  • Lust: Increases attributes and drop enemies abilities.
  • Wrath: Enhances a warrior's damage, or directly hit enemies.
  • Pride: Raises the fort's happiness.
  • Greed: Increases productivity.
  • Sloth: Upgrade pets.
  • Gluttony: Consumes ressources to heal or boost a succubi's health.
  • Envy: Take control over enemies.
  • Fire: Burn enemies and causes a lot of damage everywhere.
Those rituals may cause minor sides effects including irritability, personality alterations, increase in body fat, sudden thirst or learning of skills. You may notice that only if you look closely at your workers.

Worker upgrades does not require any skills, those reactions are designed to affect a selected worker. You can use the workshop profile (shift + P with the temple selected) to select who will receive the bonus.

Temple of sin -

1 block		 11x11 Craftsuccubi, butchers, well of soul

The temple of sin provides upgrades to your succubi.

  • Learn martial excitement (lust): Increases combat abilities based on happiness. Armok may have mercy of your enemies if they meet an ecstatic succubus.
  • Learn pheromones + entice: The succubus becomes a courtesan and receive access to several interactions that affect intelligent creatures. Her enemies will have trouble raising a sword at her. At regular intervals she will see her speed multiplied.
  • Learn berserk rage (wrath): Increases combat abilities based on unhappiness. It will still provide a small boost if the succubus is happy. If she is miserable however...
  • Devour soul (gluttony): Eat a soul to receive a slight increase in physical abilities.
  • Learn abyssal gaze (lust): Render an enemy unable to think straight, they may even fall unconscious after a while.
  • Learn necrosis chant (wrath): An enemy is hit by nausea and necrosis. This interaction has a long recharge time.

Here you can unleash spells that affect the entire site.

  • Forget death (pride): Causes the succubi to forget recent tragedies. The more friendships the succubus casting this ritual has, the more thoughts are removed.
  • Protective tentacles (sloth): Tentacle monsters will increase the defense of nearby succubi for two months.

 Sorcery 1 statue,

The abyssal temple allow you to reach for the fools who dare to attack your fort to suggest them unwise actions. Failing that, you can also bind spells at them from the comfort of your dungeon.

  • Lure invaders inside (envy): Siegers will break camp and all run into your fort. This is useful if your defenses are ready and you do not wish to wait further.
  • Sow discord (envy): Playing on internal grudges, you can cause invaders to fight each other to death. Invaders resume their siege after dealing with their traitors.
  • Dimension pull (wrath): Slam a few invaders on the ground, causing damage.

 Diplomacy 1 statue,

The altar of nightmares allow the succubi to visit the mortals in their dreams and whisper them new desires. This can entice leaders into making bad decisions this way or lead important characters into revealing their secrets.

  • Lure caravan: Call down a caravan from hostile civs for you to butcher and loot.
  • Visit brokers/crafters/soldier dreams: Learn a skill based on the target.
  • Provoke a siege: Offend  another civ and provoke an attack. The attack might occur from any hostile race outside their season. If no immediate effect is seen, you may also have an ambush incoming.

Temple of fire -


Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Sorcery
 Sorcery 1 statue 5x7 Soul well, abyssal temple

The temple of fire is great for those who wish to turn everything into ashes. You will find both upgrades and site wide effect here.

  • Become fire immune: Turn a succubus into a fireborn succubi, unaffected by fire.
  • Learn gehenna fire: The worker becomes a pyromancer and can throw fireballs at long range or breath fire at close quarters.
  • Learn torrid kiss: Grants a melee fire attack. This attack is free and will not interupt combat.
  • Conjure fire storm: Summons dozens of fireballs to strike the outside area. Twice.

Den of iniquity -


Build labor Skill Buildmat Dimension Requirements
Sorcery
 Sorcery 1 bed,
1 whip		 3x3 Cage traps, Still, Screw press

The den of iniquity is where your succubi will charm their prisoners and convince them to join your fort. The effect is different depending of the target.

To charm a prisoner, you must first install him or her near your den of iniquity. The most simple is to build its cage nearby, assuming that it ran into a trap. Chain also work, but roaming enemieswill frighten the succubus.

  • Corrupt prisoner: Seduce a sentient character, to join your civilization. It does not stop there as the succubi infuse a part of her energy into the prisoner, transforming him or her into a hybrid. This affect civilized creatures not opposed to life. These hybrids are mostly not as good at sorcery and animal training as pure succubi.
    • Humans turn into devils who are good traders.
    • Dwarves turn into fiends, good miners and fighters.
    • Elves and drows turn into cambions, good ambushers and archers.
    • Goblins turn into hellions, good lashers who do not need to eat.
    • Kobolds turn into imps, excellent dodgers and knife users.
    • Warlocks cannot be turned under normal conditions.
    • Other races default into one of the castes above.
  • Charm minotaur: Seduce a minotaur into joining your civilization. They do not follow orders and stay around the living areas, but will beat up any intruders.

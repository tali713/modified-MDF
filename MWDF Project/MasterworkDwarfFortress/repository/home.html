Welcome to MDF!

This is the manual for Masterwork that will guide you through Initial Setup, starting your first fortress, and will provide information about changes and additions the mod has to offer; each section can be seen on the nav-bar on the left side of the page.

Quick References:

  • Sections under Welcome to MDF! will give you an overview of the features included in Masterwork. You can view the launcher GUI manual, view links to other players forts, and get an introductory view of the different civilizations. This section also includes a quick guide to the new world-gen additions..

  • Sections under RAW Additions will show you the specific objects added to the game. Creatures, Civilizations, Inorganics, Grass types, Plant types, and the various tilesets can be reviewed in greater detail here.

  • Sections under Extra Content contains an overview of third-party applications included with Masterwork, as well as links to their respective threads and readme files.

  • The Playable Civs section gives you an overview of all currently playable civilizations. Each civ offers a unique playstyle and variety of both strengths and weaknesses. It's definitely worth trying them all at least once!

  • If you'd like to support Meph, the original author of Masterwork Dwarf Fortress, it is possible to do so through the donation link. Please remember that both Masterwork and Dwarf Fortress are both free!

  • If you have specific questions, feedback, would like to show your support, or have anything you want to share with the community, you can do so on the bay12 Masterwork DF Forums. You might also want to visit the Dwarf Fortress Subreddit, or join us on the Modified Masterwork Dwarf Fortress Discord!

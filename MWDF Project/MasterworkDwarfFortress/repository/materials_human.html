Materials

Humans dont have any new or unique materials; instead they have access to everything all other races can make through their traders.

Of the modded materials, humans can make:

- Bonemold, an armor-only iron-grade material made from bonemeal and ash. Done by the Alchemist.

- Bloodsteel, a steel-grade metal made from blood, bonemold, ash and flux. Done by the Alchemist.

- Mithril, Tungsten and Cobalt in the Crucible, just like dwarves.

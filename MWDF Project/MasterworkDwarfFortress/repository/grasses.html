Grasses

Surface grass is uniform, there is only one type of grass. Only exception is bamboo, for your pandas.

Good region grass will make your grazing animals stronger.

Evil region grass will make your grazing animals go berserk.

The first cavern is covered in moss.

The second cavern is covered in crystals. It gives your domestic pets a defense bonus if eaten.

The third cavern is covered in floor tiles, simulating an old ruin/dungeon.

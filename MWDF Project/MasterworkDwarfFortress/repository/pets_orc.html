General info:

  • All creatures can be milked twice as often as in vanilla.
  • All poisonous/venomous creatures can be milked for their poison/venom.
  • Many creatures give fleece when sheared, giving 3 units of cloth after spinning.
  • Many creatures give pelts when butchered, giving furs after tanning.
  • All animals give leather relative of their size. A cat gives 1, a oxen gives 7.
  • All animals lay way, way less eggs.

Orc Pets

Orcs do not have as many pets as most other races, but what they do have, is pretty unique. Most of the animals are designed to survive in harsh climates and make good warbeasts. Condors hunt vermin, while the rest of the pets help in combat.

::: Direwolf ::: Price: 51 :::
A large predator, trainable in both war or hunting. They breed relatively quick and form the basis of orcish warbeasts.

::: Steppe Aurochs ::: Price: 61 :::
Grazing pack and pull animal, you will most likely embark with two of those in front of your wagon. They make good sources of meat and leather and can even survive quite a beating.

::: Shaggy Mumak ::: Price: 501 :::
Essentially a mammoth with six tusks, this great beast makes both a good source of meat as well as mayhem. Due to their size they make excellent warbeasts.

::: Taiga Sabrecat ::: Price: 126 :::
A fast moving predator, trainable in both war or hunting.

::: Arctic Condor::: Price: 51 :::
A flying hunting animals, great as pets for your hunters to chase down other birds. They can also be farmed for their eggs.

Special: Hunts vermin.

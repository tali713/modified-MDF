Materials

Dwarves have access to several new metals and leathers that are pretty unique. Master metalsmiths and forgers, they can work adamantine and make clothing from gold, silver or mithril strands.

Volcanic is made in the volcanic foundry. Its better than steel , but is very expensive and requires magma.

Tungsten is a new weapon/armor material that is heavy, but excellent for blunt weapons.

Cobalt is an alternative to iron.

Titanium is a lightweight and valuable decorational material, but very brittle.

Orichalcum is extracted slowly, 1% from each ore, but makes for excellent weapons and armor, or for rare magical reactions. Use it wisely. Artefacts from it are prized possessions.

The tanner can tan chitin, shell and scales; improve them to chitinplate, shellplate and scaleplate, which are about iron-grade. You can also harden leather, which is twice as strong as normal leather.

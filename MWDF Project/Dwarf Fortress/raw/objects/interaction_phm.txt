Basically it works like this:
One PHM in rebellion mode targets one or more creatures.
This gives all target creatures the ability to stare down 5 PHM.
One tick later, all nearby PHM gain the ability to do a follow-up charge, but only if they were not stared down.
If a PHM does a follow-up charge, all the other PHM will join in.
